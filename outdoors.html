Taniti's white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay are a popular spot for both locals and visitors of the island. These beaches are perfect for relaxing by the ocean or taking a swim to cool off in the water. The island offers many water and beach activities including boat tours and snorkeling.
