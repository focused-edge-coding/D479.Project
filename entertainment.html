Helicopter

Enjoy an aerial view of Taniti Island with a helicopter tour. This unique exerience is one of the most popular activities amoung visitors of the island.

Taniti Island Helicopter Tours offers many different options from group tours to private bookings. They offer different tour lengths which vary in price.

Visitors should book their tour in advance on their website.
These tours often sell out quickly during peak times.
