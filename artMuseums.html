Art and Museums

Figure 10:
Art gallery

Many local artists display their unique works for sale in local art galleries. These galleries are open to the public.

The galleries often host art showcases to display specific types of art or art from a specific artist.

The Oceanside Art Showcase

This was a popular art showcase that took place in 2024. The showcase displayed ocean and beach themed art by local artist. Some of the most popular pieces included a seashell pottery sculpture, a painting of a little girl on the beach at sunset, and an aerial view painting of the island.
