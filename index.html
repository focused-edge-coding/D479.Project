Welcome to Taniti Island

Taniti is the perfect place for a tropical vacation!

Figure 2:
Palm trees with pink sunset

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. Come see our white sandy beaches, tropical rainforests, and small active volcano.


Taniti City boasts native architecture with white sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Many activities are located in Marriton Landing which is located on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.


Taniti City and Merriton Landing are both easy to explore on foot. Visitors can rent bikes and helmets on the island.


Activies for visitors of all ages.
Visit the local history museum and art galleries.
Enjoy helicopter rides and chartered fishing tours.
Snorkle in the ocean and zip-line in the rainforest.
Hike to see the small active volcano.
