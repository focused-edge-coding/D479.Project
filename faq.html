Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What power outlets are used on the island?
    The same as the United States. 120V power outlets

  2. What is the legal drinking age on the island?
    The drinking age is 18. This is not strictly enforced.

  3. When is alcohol served?
    Alcohol is NOT allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9 a.m.

  4. Do the resident speak English?
    Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. In rural areas, residents speak very little English, especially by the older residents

  5. Are healthcare facilities accessible to visitors?
    There is one hospital and several clinics on the island. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

  6. Is there a high crime rate on the island?
    Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but an increase in tourism has increased the number of reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes

  7. Are attractions and restaurants open on holidays?
    Taniti celebrates many national holidays. Many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays. Visitors are encouraged to contact specific attractions and restaurants for specific information regarding holiday hours.

  8. What is the local currency?
    Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many business will accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange. Many businesses accept major credit cards.
