Figure 12:
Beach sunset with couple silhouette

Taniti's white sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay are beautiful during sunrise and sunset. Reserve your picnic kit from the Taniti Island Resort Restaurant. Couples enjoy the local shops and activites at Merriton Landing. Merriton Landing Boat Tours offers private boat tours that many couples book.
