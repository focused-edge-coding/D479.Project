Night Life

Figure 9:
Spotlight party scene

The Taniti Island Dance Club is a new party spot for people to enjoy dancing on the island. There is a DJ on-site every night, and there is way to request songs on their website. They serve alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages and have some food options. This is a great place to visit if you enjoy a great party.


The dance club requires visitors to be 18 and older due to alcohol being sold in the club.
